JioMart Distributor Registration: How to become a JioMart Franchise, Seller, Vendor

July 11, 2020July 11, 2020 by Jiomartshop

JioMart Distributor Registration: How to get JioMart Franchise, Distributor, Seller, Kirana? and How to do Business with JioMart website and app.

Jio is coming up with e-commerce company Jio Mart to and first, they are stepping into the online grocery market. With the launch of JioMart, the company has started preparing for operations. In the initial stage, Jio Mart service is available only in Navi-Mumbai, Kalyan and Thane and later it will expand to the whole country. The JioMart Distributor registration is also opened by the company to all the interested businessman.

The registration for Jio Mart is open to all the people who are looking to become a Jio Mart distributor. Here’s the complete detail on the JioMart Distributor registration.

If you are a retailer or shop owner and looking to do business with JioMart then JIO MART is giving an opportunity to all the vendors to register on Jio Mart website. Here are some of the points to keep in mind before registering for the Jio Mart distributorship.

  • JioMart distributor must be currently doing distribution business with a renowned brand
  • JioMart distributor must-have the infrastructure for doing business.
  • JioMart distributor must have finances
  • JioMart distributor must have a good relationship with the retailers

If you want to become JioMart distributor then you must fulfil all the above norms and then it will be approved by the company sales team. 

What are the benefits of JioMart Franchise?

You must be having this question in your mind that why to become the JioMart distributor? Jio is one of the biggest company in India and had brought the revolution in the telecom industry. 

If you will become JioMart distributor then you will become a partner of one of the fastest-growing company. You will get benefits like high commission along with with other facilities JioMart offers, high-end technology, GST related helps, discounts on Jio services and many more.

Documents Required for JioMart Distributorship

  • Two passport size photographs
  • Voter ID or Aadhar card photocopy
  • Address document
  • Firm certificates
  • PAN Card
  • GST certificate

How to Become a JioMart Distributor?

If you fulfil all the norms of the Jio Mart dealership and have all the required documents then register for the Jio Mart Distributor. Here are the steps you should follow to become the JioMart Distributor.

  1. Visit the official website by Clicking Here.
  2. Then, Click on the “I am interested” button.
  3. Fill all your personal details such as Name, Firm Name, Email, Address, City, Pincode, and Mobile Number.
  4. Verify the captcha.
  5. Click on Submit button
  6. You will be displayed “Your lead created successfully”.

You have successfully submitted your entry for the JioMart Distributor.
