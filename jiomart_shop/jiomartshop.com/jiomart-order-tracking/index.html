JioMart Order Tracking: How to Track Jio Mart Order, Status, Delivery Date & Time

June 1, 2020June 1, 2020 by Jiomartshop

Reliance Jio has launched its dream project JioMart e-commerce website in the country. The operation was initially serviceable in Mumbai region but now it’s available in more than 200 cities across the country. If you have placed an order on Jio Mart but do not know the order status, delivery date and time then here’s how you can do JioMart order tracking.

The E-commerce platform JioMart allow customers to purchase groceries and daily essentials through its website. The Jiomart app will be soon available on Apple App Store and Google play store.

Customers can order the products online through their WhatsApp number if the WhatsApp service is available for their pin code. After the announcement of Mark Zuckerberg’s Facebook purchased 10% per cent share in Reliance Jio. Jiomart allowed its customer to place an order via WhatsApp number.

JioMart Order Tracking: How to Track Jio Mart Order

Here are the steps to follow to track your JioMart order status, delivery date and time.

Step 1: First, visit the JioMart website (www.jiomart.com) and then enter your PIN code to find out that this service is available in your city.

Step 2: If it’s available in your area then sign-in to the website.

Step 3: Then, enter your mobile number and get the OTP.

Step 4: Then, enter all your details, such as email id, name, password and phone number.

Step 5: After the successful registration to the website, Search the item or product that you want to buy and add them into the cart.

Step 6: Click on the proceed button.

Step 7: Add the address and other details such as PIN code, mobile number and save it.

Step 8: Make payment via wallets, net banking, debit, and credit cards.

Step 9: You will message from the Jiomart that your order placed.

Step 10: You can now check your account under which you will see order, click on it to track your Jiomart order status and delivery date.

Note: As Jiomart is in its intial phase and due to corona virus there may be delay in deliver but products will be delivered within 48 hours.
