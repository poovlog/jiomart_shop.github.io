JioMart Franchise Registration: How to Apply for the Jiomart Franchise?

JioMart website is now live for all the customers who want to purchase the groceries product from the Jiomart.com on discount rates. The service was earlier available in Maharashtra only but now it has expanded to over 200 cities in India. If you are planning to apply for the franchise then here are the details for the JioMart franchise registration.

The company will now compete with old players like BigBasket and Grofers. Reliance is a very powerful company which makes Jiomart a successful venture in the initial start.

JioMart Franchise Registration

The registration for the Jiomart franchise is not yet started for the interested participants. If you are looking to become the distributors of the Jiomart then you can register on the Jio’s official website.

Documents

If you want to become a Jio mart franchise then there are certain criteria that you have to fulfil. You must be a resident of India with all the valid proofs.

The registration for the Jiomart franchise is not yet started but these are the document that would is required while doing registration.

  • GST certificate
  • PAN card
  • Aadhar Card
  • Photographs
  • Voter ID card
  • Company/Firm Registration Papers

Returns or ROI

There is not much information about the return of investment (ROI) of Jiomart but there is a high possibility that you would get good returns of the investment.

Reliance is known for the trust and vision and Jiomart will become one of the top e-commerce sites in India and their distributor will surely earn a good income from the franchise partnership.
